Rigid, still, weight forward on the lead foot, IR lasers painting the opposing doorframes (including the curtained northwest bedroom with the faint amber bulb). - Ankle-height dust drift continues to ribbon across the hard-packed dirt of the inner courtyard around the white Hilux and the south gate; a faint amber rectangle of light still bleeds from the 2nd-floor northwest window of `A1`. The weak orange bulb over the south vehicle gate throws its tiny dim circle on the dirt.
