# Snapshot 04 — Breach

**Timecode:** 0:18 – 0:24
**Beat:** Squad Leader gives the go. AI fans out the compressed order to the Breacher and the four-man entry stack simultaneously. The linear charge detonates on the front door of main residence `A1` inside compound `AC-1`'s inner courtyard. The door blasts inward. Alpha is through the threshold on the first frame after the thump. This is the money beat for "one human intent → many synchronized executions."

## Camera & Movement
- **Wide low-angle, 24mm-equivalent, composed for 2.39:1 anamorphic letterbox crop.** Camera planted at ankle height on the hard-packed dirt of the inner courtyard, roughly 2.5 m back from the front door of main residence `A1`, **45° off the door hinge** so the viewer sees both the door plane and the breach stack edge-on.
- Tripod base with a short slider; the shot rides a subtle 0.1 m/s push-in that holds steady through detonation — no handheld jitter pre-blast.
- **Shutter judder for one frame only on the detonation** (the 180° shutter drops to ~45° for a single exposure), giving the door-failure instant a harder, stuttered "film gate slipped" feel without turning the shot into a shake-cam.
- **Subtle anamorphic horizontal flare** streaks from the breach spark across the frame at eye level — amber, thin, with a soft lens-element bokeh halo.
- Mild rolling-shutter smear on the SureFire weapon-light cones as they sweep through the dust cloud.
- Volumetric haze thickens the NVG IR laser lines so the DBAL-I2 beams read as solid pencils of light stabbing into the breach.

## Action in Frame
- **Pre-blast (first ~0.3 s):** the Breacher's gloved hand is still on the detonator clacker at the jamb of main residence `A1`, his 10.3" URG-I slung muzzle-down across his chest on a Magpul MS4. Operator `A1` (on point) is hard against the weathered cream stucco wall behind him, NVGs flipped down, IR laser on, rifle low-ready. Behind operator `A1`, TL-A's helmet edge and the orange IR chemlight on the **Squad Leader's** helmet glow hot at the back of the stack — the only visual tell of rank.
- **Detonation frame:** the linear charge fires on the front door of main residence `A1`. The door blasts inward in a violent cone of splinters, peeled concrete/stucco dust, and splintered door panels boiling in a grey-white cloud. Warm orange detonation light spills laterally onto the weathered cream stucco wall of `A1` and rim-lights the Breacher's helmet from below. Ankle-height courtyard dust is momentarily displaced in a concentric shockwave ring pushing outward across the hard-packed dirt.
- **Post-blast (~0.4 s in):** the **Breacher** transitions in one smooth motion — clacker drops on a retention lanyard, strong hand sweeps back to the pistol grip of his slung M4, muzzle comes up. He crashes through the breach first, NVGs down, weapon light cutting a solid white cone through the dust cloud into `A1`'s interior hallway — dark, peeling plaster walls, worn concrete floor, a faint low-wattage exposed bulb visible deeper in.
- Operator **`A1`** (on point) follows on the Breacher's back, left shoulder almost touching, muzzle stacked high over the Breacher's, DBAL-I2 IR laser painting the interior hallway.
- **Flashbang** arcs past the lens from screen-right toward `A1`'s interior hallway, mid-air, pin already pulled, spoon tumbling free — thrown by operator `A2` from deeper in the stack. It catches one hot specular hit of amber from the dying detonation.
- **Helmet IR strobes** pulse on the back plates of the Breacher and operator `A1` — thin anamorphic flares on each pulse.
- Courtyard context visible in the periphery: the battered white Toyota Hilux parked askew, the single-story cinderblock annex `A2` in the southwest corner of the courtyard, and the weak orange bulb over the south vehicle gate throwing its dim circle on the dirt. Small barred windows on `A1`'s stucco facade rattle from the overpressure.

## Diegetic HUD Overlay (holographic, layered ON the image)
The HUD floats directly on the frame as a transparent holographic AR layer — not a solid panel, not a split-screen. Think "drone sensor view" or "command-net repeater" with semi-transparent glyphs, subtle bloom, and micro-scanlines. All glyphs are anchored in negative space (overcast sky above `A1`'s parapet, upper corners of the weathered cream stucco wall, dark hard-packed dirt of the inner courtyard) so they never cover the operators, the detonation, the flashbang, or the doorway.

- **Upper-third, pale green `#7FFFA9`, thin monospace all-caps, luminous, heaviest bloom** — raw leader intent appears first, keyed to the whispered "three, two, one":
  ```
  [LEADER → AI]
  "BREACHER GREEN LIGHT, FLASHBANG ON MY GO"
  ```
- **Mid-upper, dim amber `#FFB347`, thinner weight, subtle CRT scanline** — AI routing metadata flickers in underneath as the fan-out resolves, just before the charge fires:
  ```
  [AI ROUTING]  INTENT PARSED → FAN-OUT: BREACHER + 4 ENTRY NODES
  ```
- **Lower-third, bright cyan-white `#EAFBFF`, heavier bloom, thin rule above and below each line** — the compacted summaries land in the earpieces of the addressed nodes, stacked in order of receipt:
  ```
  ──────────────────────────────────────
  [BREACHER EARPIECE]    "GREEN LIGHT."
  [ENTRY TEAM EARPIECE]  "FLASHBANG ON LEADER'S GO."
  ──────────────────────────────────────
  ```
- **Top-right corner micrographic:** the 10-node command-tree reference. The **SL** node pulses pale green at the moment the leader speaks. A heartbeat later, the **BREACHER** node and the four entry nodes (**TL-A, A1, A2, TL-B**-relay) pulse bright cyan in a single synchronized frame as the routed summaries land. The **OVER** (sniper) node is visible on the tree, dim/steady, not pulsing this frame.
- **Bottom-left timestamp:** `03:02:52` in thin amber mono.
- **Bottom-right:** `TACNET` wordmark lockup at 40% opacity.
- HUD glyphs sit exclusively in the sky above `A1`'s flat roof parapet and along the dark hard-packed dirt of the courtyard foreground; nothing is ever painted over the door, the Breacher, operator `A1`, the flashbang, or the detonation flare.

## Audio
- Whispered countdown on the Squad Leader's breath, tight on the boom mic: "three… two… one."
- Muffled concussive **thump** of the linear charge — low frequency, chest-punch, with a crisp dry splinter crackle tail as `A1`'s front door comes apart.
- Short metallic **pin-pull ping** of the flashbang as it leaves operator `A2`'s hand, followed by the aluminum spoon flicking free and tinking once against the blown door frame as it passes through.
- Suppressed carbine sling slap, one sharp boot impact on the threshold as the Breacher crosses it.
- A single soft electronic "confirm" chime on each earpiece delivery (two chimes, cyan-white tone) layered under the Breacher's "Green light." readback.
- Ankle-height courtyard dust drift and thin high-altitude wind bed continuous underneath. No music.
