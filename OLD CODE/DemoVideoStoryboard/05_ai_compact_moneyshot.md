# Snapshot 05 — 4 Voices → 1 Clean Line (MONEY SHOT)

**Timecode:** 0:24 – 0:30
**Beat:** The defining moment. Four Alpha operators report simultaneously from four different ground-floor rooms deep inside the main residence `A1` of the fortified walled compound `AC-1`. TacNet compacts the messy overlapping chatter into a single clean synthesized SITREP delivered to the Squad Leader's earpiece. This single hero frame — and the audio cut paired with it — is the entire product pitch. It must be the most polished image in the film.

## Camera & Movement
- **Over-the-shoulder of the Squad Leader (SL)** standing just inside the breached front doorway of main residence `A1`, holding rear security back into the inner courtyard of compound `AC-1`.
- He is in **profile silhouette**, camera right-of-axis, foreground ear and cheek sharp with the ComTac earpiece and boom mic in crisp detail; the orange IR chemlight on the back of his helmet softly haloed.
- **Lens:** 85mm, T1.8, **shallow depth of field** — SL rim-lit against the dark hallway interior of A1, background operators rendered as creamy anamorphic bokeh and flash fragments.
- **Micro-movement:** an almost imperceptible 3% push-in across the 6 seconds, plus a tiny handheld breath (~1° jitter) to keep it alive; no obvious dolly, no whip.
- Shot is held static on his profile — the drama lives in his micro-reaction and in the HUD resolving around his helmet, not in camera motion.
- **Foreground:** the splintered A1 door frame on camera left (concrete-stucco blown inward, chunks of weathered cream plaster hanging, rebar stubs exposed, faint smoke curling); SL's shoulder and slung 10.3" URG-I M4 cropping the right third of the frame, suppressor pointing down and out toward the inner courtyard behind him for rear security.
- **Background, through the open breached A1 doorway looking outward into the compound's inner courtyard behind him** (soft bokeh, past SL's shoulder): the battered white Toyota Hilux parked askew on hard-packed dirt, the single-story cinderblock annex `A2` silhouetted in the southwest corner of the courtyard, and, much deeper, the weak orange glow of the bare incandescent bulb above the south vehicle gate. No asphalt, no fog — only ankle-height dust drift catching cold teal-green ambient light.
- **Background, through the same doorway looking inward into A1's dark interior** (the other depth layer, across the threshold): a dark hallway with peeling plaster walls, worn bare concrete floor, a low-wattage exposed incandescent bulb swinging on a bare wire, dust particulate floating thick in the air, and flickering SureFire weapon-light cones cutting through it as Alpha operators sweep ground-floor rooms further in. Warm suppressed muzzle flashes pop as soft amber blooms in the far back.

## Action in Frame
- The Squad Leader stands rock-still in the breached A1 doorway, weapon low-ready angled rearward into the courtyard, **head tilted a few degrees toward the earpiece side**, **eyes half-closed for a single beat** as he listens.
- His free hand rests lightly on the pistol grip; the index finger indexed flat along the receiver.
- Four Alpha operators deeper inside A1's ground floor are reporting simultaneously over their boom mics from four different rooms — their voices overlapping, messy, stepping on each other. We only see fragments of them: a shoulder crossing a doorway, a muzzle light sweeping a peeling-plaster wall, a boot at the base of an interior concrete staircase, the silhouette of a stack clearing a kitchen threshold.
- On the single clean compacted line landing in SL's earpiece, he gives a **millimeter-level micro-reaction**: a faint tightening of the jaw, a nearly invisible single-frame nod. No dialogue from him. No head turn. Just that.
- Ankle-height dust drifts in thin ribbons across the breached threshold behind him, lit cold by the moonless overcast ambient from the courtyard; it meets warm amber interior light bleeding from deeper A1 rooms — the two palettes touching right across his profile.

## Diegetic HUD Overlay (holographic, layered ON the image)
The HUD is a **transparent holographic AR layer projected onto the image** — as if rendered by a drone sensor or the viewer's optic. It is **NOT a split-screen panel** and **NOT a solid block**. Thin monospace, ALL CAPS, +20 tracking, subtle CRT scanlines, ~2 s micro glitch, Gaussian bloom, only 3–5% darkened backing behind glyph footprints for legibility. Every glyph is anchored in negative space (the overcast sky above the A1 door frame, the dark hallway void of A1 behind SL, the splintered door frame itself, the black corners of the hallway darkness) — **nothing covers SL's face, his earpiece, his weapon, or the operators visible through the doorway.**

Color & motion rules for this stack:
- The four `[OPn → AI]` raw lines render in **luminous pale green `#7FFFA9`**, stacked and **pulsing** in overlapping rhythm (each line ticks on slightly offset from the next, mimicking the overlapping audio), then **visibly fade down** to ~20% opacity as the compacted line resolves.
- `[AI ROUTING] 4 raw reports → 1 compacted SITREP` sits between them in **dim amber `#FFB347`**, thinner weight, with a subtle progress tick scrubbing left-to-right.
- `[LEADER EARPIECE]` resolves last in **bright cyan-white `#EAFBFF`** with **heavier Gaussian bloom** and a **thin horizontal rule above and below**; it **glows up** as the green lines fade, so the eye lands there exactly when the audio lands there.
- **Top-right corner:** the 10-node TacNet command-tree micrographic (same constant as every shot). The four Alpha operator nodes `A1 / A2 / TL-A / BREACHER` pulse pale green in sync with the raw lines, then collapse inward along routing edges into a single bright cyan-white line that terminates at the `SL` node — a literal visual of "4 → 1". The `OVER` (sniper, on the distant ridge, not in frame here) node is visible on the tree, steady and dimmer, for command-picture continuity.
- **Bottom-left timestamp:** `03:03:02`

## Audio
- **Defining audio cut of the entire film — THE audio edit that sells the entire product.** Four overlapping whispered operator voices — hot, messy, stepping on each other, mic'd close with boom-mic proximity breath and fabric rustle, each from a different A1 ground-floor room acoustic (concrete-floor foyer slap, plaster-walled living room, interior stairwell flutter echo, tiled kitchen).
- At the moment `[AI ROUTING]` ticks, the four raw voices **duck hard** (fast -18 dB dip, ~80 ms) under a single clean synthesized TTS earpiece line: **"First floor 90% clear, 1 EKIA. Team 2 in contact upstairs."** — one consistent slightly warm neutral voice, not robotic, with a tight close-mic earpiece character (no room). This four-voices-collapse-to-one-voice edit is what the whole film is built around.
- Underneath: faint suppressed `pfft-pfft` from the far back of A1's ground floor, plaster dust settling, a single shell casing tinking onto the concrete floor somewhere off-camera, a distant generator hum bleeding in from the courtyard shed, the SL's own controlled nasal exhale.
- **No music.** No radio static bursts. The mix itself — four voices → one voice — is the product pitch.
