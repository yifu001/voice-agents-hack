## Action in Frame

### Phase A — Exfil Bird's-Eye (0:54 – 0:58)
The full 10-man element flows out of the front door of main residence `A1`, crosses the inner courtyard (past the battered white Toyota Hilux parked askew, past the rusted diesel generator shed), and exits through the **west pedestrian gate** in a tight **inverted wedge**, moving as a single organism toward the waiting exfil vehicles just off-frame at the bottom of the composition.

- **The rescued hostage** sits dead center of the wedge, wrapped in an operator-issued coat (multicam-black shell, oversized on her frame). Her feet are moving but both elbows are clearly supported — **the Medic on her left, one Bravo operator (B1) on her right**, each with a firm cupped hand under her arm.
- **Alpha element (SL, TL-A, A1-op, A2-op, Breacher)** forms the forward arc of the wedge, suppressed 10.3" URG-I M4s still up and scanning outward along the approach path ahead. DBAL-I2 IR lasers paint thin needle-lines forward into the dust drift toward the dry-stone wall and the irrigation ditches.
- **Bravo element (TL-B, B2) plus the reintegrating Sniper `OVER`** form the rearward arc, weapons up and covering back toward main residence `A1` and the east flank in case of squirters.
- **The Squad Leader** — identifiable by the single orange IR chemlight glowing on the back of his Ops-Core FAST — walks at the very back edge of the formation, body turned slightly rear-facing, rifle low-ready.
- **The Sniper (`OVER`)**, carrying his suppressed Mk22-pattern DMR at a low patrol carry, has descended from the flat roof of the small isolated farm outbuilding on the elevated ridge ~320 m NNE and is **re-integrating into the formation through the west pedestrian gate** from the same approach path Element Alpha used in shot 01, moving in at a controlled jog.
- **Ankle-height dust drift** displaces around ten pairs of Salomon Forces boots on the hard-packed dirt, leaving faint trails curling behind each operator as they move.
- **Annex `A2`** is silhouetted in the southwest corner of the courtyard; the bare orange incandescent bulb over the south steel vehicle gate still glows faintly in the far corner of the frame.
- The curtained 2nd-floor northwest window of `A1` that was amber-lit earlier in the film is now dark — the structure is cold.
- GPNVG-18 tubes are **flipped up** on every helmet now that the element is in open air and exfiltrating.
