# Snapshot 08 — Upstairs Contact

**Timecode:** 0:42 – 0:48
**Beat:** Kinetic pivot. OP5 (Alpha element, fifth man) crosses the threshold of the 2nd-floor northwest bedroom of the main residence `A1` inside the fortified compound `AC-1` and makes contact on a single armed tango braced over the hooded hostage. Two clean suppressed rounds end the threat without the precious cargo (PC) being grazed. TacNet instantly kicks the contact up the tree to the Squad Leader. The raid's most kinetic second has to read as surgical, not messy.

## Action in Frame
Interior of a dim 2nd-floor northwest bedroom of the main residence `A1` inside compound `AC-1`. The room is maybe 3 x 4 meters: plaster walls with visible cracks and peeling paint, a worn concrete floor partly covered by a threadbare rug shoved to one side, a stained mattress laid directly on the floor against the north wall, a single small **deep-set barred window** on the west wall with a thin curtain, a tipped-over nightstand, a low-wattage exposed bulb hanging from a cord from the ceiling, dust particulate drifting through the still air.

- A hostile male in dirty civilian clothes — stained olive pants, a faded gray henley, a ratty chest rig holding a single AKM and two spare mags — is braced over a kneeling, zip-tied, hooded hostage slumped forward on the mattress on the floor. The hostage's wrists are bound behind, head down in the black cotton hood, shoulders trembling but motionless otherwise.
- The tango is mid-turn toward the doorway, AKM coming up off the hostage's back, eyes catching the weapon-light an instant too late.
- Two clean suppressed rounds from OP5's 10.3" URG-I M4 (SOCOM Mini 2 suppressor) impact the tango's upper chest — a controlled pair, tight vertical spacing. Warm orange muzzle flashes bloom against the teal-green night palette, brass glints gold as it ejects and tumbles toward the concrete floor. No gore, no spray — the hit reads entirely through flash, recoil, and the tango's collapse.
- The tango is thrown back and away from the hostage, body already falling out of frame toward the mattress's foot; the AKM slips out of his hands and clatters onto the concrete floor ahead of him.
- The hostage doesn't flinch — head still down inside the hood. A single labored breath moves the shoulders once.
- The small deep-set barred west window leaks a faint dim amber exterior light from a distant source; the curtain billows once from the concussion of the earlier breach and settles.
- Plaster dust drifts through the weapon-light cone. One shell casing rolls into the foreground across the concrete, glinting.
