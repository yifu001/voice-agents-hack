## Narrative Purpose
This 6-second bridge is the dynamic-entry montage compressed — the connective tissue between "door goes" and "AI cleans up the chatter." Shot 04 closes on the violent instant of controlled chaos: the linear charge popping A1's front door, the Breacher and operator A1 crashing through, a flashbang arcing toward the hallway. Shot 05 opens on the inverse — the Squad Leader rock-still in profile at that same breached doorway, eyes half-closed, already the calm pole of the entire assault. The bridge has to carry the viewer from the violence of penetration to the stillness of command, so that by the time the four-voices-duck-under-one-clean-AI-line edit hits on the end frame, we understand what that stillness means: Alpha is already deep inside A1 clearing rooms, and the SL is the node TacNet is compacting everything back to. The bridge also seeds the viewer with micro-glimpses of the ground-floor rooms (foyer, living room, kitchen threshold) that will be referenced in shot 05's HUD report lines, so the audience has an unconscious spatial memory of the spaces being called clear.
