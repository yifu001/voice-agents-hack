# Snapshot 02 — Sniper Overwatch Check-In

**Timecode:** 0:06 – 0:12
**Beat:** First demonstration of bubble-up: a sniper's raw, whispered heat-signature call is compressed by TacNet into a single clean synthesized line in the squad leader's earpiece. The viewer sees the full transformation — input, routing, output — layered as a holographic AR overlay on the thermal optic itself.

- **Thermal scope POV (white-hot palette)** for ~4.5 s through the sniper's suppressed Mk22-pattern DMR scope from ~320 m out: circular scope vignette with a crisp black iris, thin black crosshair reticle centered, fine milliradian hashes, a faint ring of chromatic bloom at the scope edge, a micro-parallax wobble of ~0.5° to sell live glass at long range across open ridge air.
- ~10x equivalent magnification through the scope, shallow "floating" depth inside the reticle ring, a breath-synced vertical drift of ~2 px over 1.5 s to imply a live shooter behind glass, with a subtle long-range parallax hint (faint heat shimmer off the distant compound, not a close adjacent wall).
- Reticle **tracks slowly left-to-right** across the three-story concrete-and-stucco façade of the main residence `A1` inside compound `AC-1` at ~3°/s, pausing for ~0.3 s on each heat signature.
- **ONE brief cut (~1.5 s) to a wide external shot** of the sniper prone on the flat roof of a small isolated farm outbuilding on elevated ridge terrain roughly 320 m north-northeast of the compound: 35mm anamorphic lens, low three-quarter rear angle, T2.0, weathered concrete roof with a low parapet, moonless overcast haze, the operator flat on his belly behind a suppressed Mk22-pattern DMR rested on a rolled shemagh across the parapet (no bipod legs deployed, no spotter), GPNVG-18 flipped down, Ops-Core FAST with IR strobe pulsing once on the back plate, thin breath vapor curling up past the rear of the receiver, hard-packed ridge ground and scrub around him.
- After the cut, return to thermal POV for the final beat as the synthesized earpiece line lands.

## Action in Frame
- Through the thermal: **two white-hot human silhouettes** standing close together in a ground-floor front room of the main residence `A1` inside compound `AC-1` (living area) — one gesturing with an arm, the other static, both holding something long and thin (long-gun heat signatures).
- **One white-hot figure pacing** in the 2nd-floor northwest bedroom of `A1`, walking a short oval path past the curtained amber-lit window, heat bleed visible through the thin concrete-stucco wall.
- **Rear of the compound completely dark** in thermal — cold walls, the annex `A2` in the southwest corner registering ambient, no heat sources along the back of `A1` or around the generator shed and chicken coop, confirming no bodies in the rear rooms or courtyard.
- Cold "negative" silhouettes of the battered white Toyota Hilux in the inner courtyard (engine long cold), the generator shed, the chicken coop, the perimeter walls with razor-wire coils, and the south steel vehicle gate read as dark shapes against the slightly warmer hard-packed dirt.
- A thin warm IR laser line from the sniper's own DBAL ghosts faintly into the scope's edge as it sweeps across the 2nd-floor NW window — a signature of live long-range target designation across 320 m of open ridge air.
- On the cutaway: the sniper's gloved trigger-finger indexed along the receiver of the Mk22-pattern DMR, lips barely moving behind the boom mic of the Peltor ComTac, the faintest condensation on the clear ballistic eye-pro.

## Diegetic HUD Overlay (holographic, layered ON the image)
The HUD is **not a split-screen panel**. Every glyph is rendered as a transparent holographic AR layer floating *on top of* the thermal image (and, for 1.5 s, on top of the rooftop cutaway), as if projected by the optic's own reticle computer. Thin monospace ALL CAPS, +20 tracking, subtle CRT scanlines, micro glitch every ~2 s, Gaussian bloom, only 3–5% darkened backing behind glyph footprints. HUD never covers a face, a weapon, the hostage house's doors, or any of the three heat signatures.

Order of appearance across the 6 seconds:

1. **t = 0.0–2.2 s** — Along the **lower third**, pinned under the scope iris, in **luminous pale green `#7FFFA9`**:
   ```
   [OVER → AI]
   "got two heat sigs front room, one upstairs NW,
    no movement rear"
   ```
   The text types in with a soft phosphor shimmer, synced to the whispered VO.

2. **t = 2.2–3.8 s** — The green line dims to ~30%. **Mid-left, in dim amber `#FFB347`**, a single compact routing line fades up:
   ```
   [AI ROUTING]  compact → priority: tactical intel → route: SL
   ```
   A faint amber data-packet squiggle travels along a hair-thin line from the sniper-side of the frame toward the top-right command tree as the text renders.

3. **t = 3.8–6.0 s** — The amber fades. **Upper third, in bright cyan-white `#EAFBFF`** with heavier Gaussian bloom and a thin hairline rule above and below the block:
   ```
   ────────────────────────────────────
   [LEADER EARPIECE]
   "Overwatch: 2 tangos front, 1 upstairs NW,
    rear clear."
   ────────────────────────────────────
   ```
   The block pulses once on the first syllable of the synthesized voice, then holds steady until the cut to shot 03.

Persistent HUD furniture across the whole 6 seconds:

- **Top-right corner:** the 10-node TacNet command-tree micrographic (SL, TL-A, A1, A2, BREACHER, TL-B, B1, B2, MEDIC, OVER). The **OVER** node pulses pale green `#7FFFA9` during beat 1, then a short amber trace animates along the branch toward **SL** during beat 2, and the **SL** node lights bright cyan-white during beat 3.
- **Bottom-left timestamp:** `03:02:28` in thin mono, dim amber.
- **Bottom-right:** the `TACNET` wordmark lockup at 40% opacity.
- A tiny floating tag **`OVER · 320 m`** pinned to the scope edge, pale green, with a hair-thin tick line to the reticle center.

## Audio
- Thin high-ridge wind across the parapet, steady, no gusts.
- Under it, the sniper's **low whisper** on the boom mic — breathy, compressed, just barely intelligible: *"got two heat sigs front room, one upstairs NW, no movement rear."*
- Immediately under the whisper, a very faint **digital hiss** as TacNet ingests the line, then a single soft confirmation click.
- Over the top, the **clean synthesized earpiece voice** — one consistent warm TTS voice — lands the compacted summary: *"Overwatch: 2 tangos front, 1 upstairs NW, rear clear."* It ducks the whisper beneath it for the final beat.
- No music, no radio static burst, no musical sting. The composed silence *is* the product.
